About Pappa Ji ka Dosa

Our Kitchen
1 Years of Excellence

A Legacy of Authentic Flavors

Founded in 1995 by Pappa Ji himself, our restaurant began as a small roadside stall serving crispy, golden dosas to early morning commuters. Today, we've grown into a beloved destination for South Indian cuisine lovers.

Our secret? We still use Pappa Ji's original recipes passed down through generations. Every dosa is made with love, fermented naturally for 24 hours, and cooked on traditional cast-iron griddles.
